Gyansha is a new venture of KCC SOFTWARE LTD. The main vision of the Gyansha is to start instructor led online tutorials

KCC SOFTWARE LTD is more then 25 year old company that aim to provide word class education in India. In this pursuit the founder of KCC Group has established world class Engineering and Management institute in Greater Noida, KCCITM .Continuing with this vision they are starting a new venture "GyanSha" that would provide Live Online Instructor Led Classes.

GyanSha is a new venture that would be providing Live Online Classes. GyanSha aims at providing classes and tutorials with ,use of technology, at anytime and anywhere. e-Learning provided by GyanSha include training, tutorials, the delivery of just-in-time information and guidance from experts.

E-learning next big thing in the education industry. E-learning helps students to learn and inhance their skill set while sitting at home and at their flexible timing. Its a service oriented sector that connects people with the wanted skill set to interact and learn from the master of that skill set, even if the master is half way around the globe. Gyansha would provide such a platform which will reduce the distance between the master and student making world truly a "Global Village" using the power of Internet.
